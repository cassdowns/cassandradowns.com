Skills

Design Systems

At the same time that terms such as DesignOps and Design Systems have emerged, my work has increasingly focussed on achieving consistency and developer parity. I'm most interested in this kind of challenge, creating a solid foundation upon which the creativity of our teams can shine.

Accessibility

I have a tremendous passion for creating design that is accessible to all. From something as simple as ensuring colourblind users can see buttons, all the way to reducing stress for those with mobility impairments. Of particular importance is plain speaking and clear instructions for users with cognitive impairments, which often fits in well with UX practices and common sense.
